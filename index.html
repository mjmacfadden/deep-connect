Directions

This app is designed, from a systems theory standpoint, to bring couples closer together who, for whatever reason aren't naturally connecting.

Set a repeating calendar reminder, twice a week, to engage in a meaningful way with your partner. Use the questions present as starting points for more deeply connected conversation.

Questions have been sourced from a variety of online resources linked below.
